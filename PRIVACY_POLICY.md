# Privacy Policy for SourceGuard

**Last Updated:** April 8, 2026

SourceGuard ("we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

* **Tenant Data:** When you register for an API key or dashboard access, we collect your name, company email, and billing information.
* **Usage Data:** We log API requests, endpoint usage, and timestamps to enforce tier quotas and monitor system performance.
* **Procurement Queries:** We process the supplier names and evaluation criteria you submit to the API to return Trust Scores.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

* To provide, maintain, and improve the API and dashboard functionality.
* To process payments and enforce monthly usage quotas.
* To communicate with you regarding security updates, tier upgrades, or service changes.

## 3. Business-to-Business (B2B) Supplier Data

SourceGuard analyzes global trade records, customs data, and public certifications. This information pertains to businesses, not individual consumers. If you believe your personal data has been inadvertently processed as part of a business entity record, please contact us for removal.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, cloud hosting) strictly for the purpose of operating our Service. 

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.
